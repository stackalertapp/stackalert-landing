Channels

Alerts wherever your team already works

Configure one or more channels per AWS account. Solo devs use Telegram, teams route to Slack, on-call engineers use PagerDuty. Mix and match.

stackalert.yaml 
notify:
  - channel: telegram
    chat_id: "@my-team-alerts"
  - channel: slack
    webhook: $SLACK_WEBHOOK_URL
  - channel: email
    to: "ops@company.com"
  - channel: pagerduty
    integration_key: $PD_KEY

# → alerts fan out to all listed channels
